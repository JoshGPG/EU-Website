# Managing News

News posts use WordPress's built-in **Posts** system with custom categories. They appear on the News page (`/news/`) in a filterable card grid.

## How to Access

1. Log in to WordPress admin
2. Click **Posts** in the left sidebar

## Adding a News Post

1. Click **Posts > Add New**
2. Fill in:
   - **Title** — the headline
   - **Editor content** — the full article
   - **Featured Image** (right sidebar) — photo shown on the news card (recommended: at least 700px wide)
   - **Category** (right sidebar) — assign to one of the pre-created categories

3. Click **Publish**

## Categories

Three categories are auto-created for the site:

| Category | Slug | Use For |
|----------|------|---------|
| Program News | `program-news` | Updates about EU programs |
| Race Event News | `race-event-news` | Race announcements, results, recaps |
| Other News | `other-news` | General announcements, community news |

The News page shows filter tabs for each category so visitors can browse by topic.

## How the News Page Works

- Posts appear in a 2-column card grid
- Each card shows: featured image (or placeholder), category label, title, date, author, excerpt, and "Read More" link
- Filter tabs at the top let visitors filter by category
- Pagination appears when there are more than 10 posts
- Posts are ordered newest first

## Tips

- Always assign a category to keep the filter tabs useful.
- Excerpts are generated automatically from the post content. To control the excerpt, fill in the **Excerpt** field (enable via Screen Options if not visible).
- Featured images make a big visual difference on the news grid. Always try to include one.
